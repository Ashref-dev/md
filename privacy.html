Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

TL;DR: We collect zero personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser. Your documents never leave your device.

Zero Data Collection

md.ashref.tn is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or documents.

All Markdown to PDF conversion happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your content never touches our servers.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your Markdown documents or content
  • Generated PDF files
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Browser fingerprints or tracking cookies
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking

Basic Usage Statistics

The only data we may collect is anonymous, aggregated usage counts through our hosting provider (standard web server logs). This includes:

  • Number of page visits (no personal identification)
  • General geographic region (country-level only)
  • Browser type and device category

This basic information helps us understand how many people use the tool, but cannot be used to identify individual users.

Local Processing

When you use md.ashref.tn, all processing happens locally in your browser:

  • Markdown parsing and rendering
  • Mermaid diagram generation
  • PDF creation and export
  • Theme preferences (stored in browser localStorage)

Nothing is uploaded to our servers or any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party resources to provide functionality:

  • Google Fonts for typography (loaded via CDN, standard caching applies)
  • CDN-hosted JavaScript libraries (markdown-it, mermaid.js, html2pdf.js)

These services may have their own privacy policies, but they do not receive any of your document content.

No Cookies

We do not use cookies. Any preferences you set (like dark mode) are stored locally in your browser using localStorage and never leave your device.

Security

Since we don't collect or store your data, there's nothing for us to lose or leak. Your documents remain on your device, under your control.

The website is served over HTTPS to ensure secure communication between your browser and our servers.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we'll update this page with the new information. Since we don't collect contact information, we can't notify you directly, so please check back periodically.
